What Evidenx is

Evidenx is an evidence-first platform focused on understanding system behavior.
Grounded in what actually happened on a system — not just signals or assumptions.

Why it exists

Many security tools prioritize speed of classification.
In incident response, clarity matters more than speed.

Engineering approach

Clarity over complexity. Traceability over opacity. Evidence before automation.
The goal is faster understanding, not faster verdicts.

Data sovereignty

Built with a European-first mindset.
Data control and deployment flexibility are core design goals.

Who it is for

Teams and organizations where evidence quality matters.
Incident response, regulated environments, insurance claim investigators, legal constraints.
